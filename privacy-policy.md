# Privacy Policy

_Effective date: [EFFECTIVE_DATE]_  
_Last updated: 2026-03-02_

This Privacy Policy explains how NodeWorks collects, uses, stores, and protects data when you use the NodeWorks Discord bot.

## 1) What Data We Collect

NodeWorks may collect and process the following data:

- Discord user IDs
- Server (guild) IDs
- Channel IDs
- Role IDs
- Command usage logs
- Bot configuration and moderation settings

**Message content is not stored by default** unless a server administrator explicitly enables a feature that requires it.

## 2) Why We Collect Data

We collect data to:

- Provide core bot functionality
- Support moderation features
- Save server-specific settings and preferences
- Maintain audit and troubleshooting logs
- Improve reliability and security of the bot

## 3) Where Data Is Stored

Data is stored in secure database and log storage systems managed by NodeWorks and/or trusted infrastructure providers.

## 4) Data Retention

- Logs and operational records are retained only as long as needed for operations, security, and troubleshooting.
- Typical log retention is **30–90 days**, depending on the data type and operational needs.
- Server settings are retained until removed, reset, or no longer required to operate the bot.

## 5) Data Sharing

NodeWorks does **not** sell personal data.

Data may be shared only:

- With service providers strictly required to operate the bot infrastructure
- If required by law, legal process, or to protect rights, safety, and security

## 7) Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data. However, no system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

## 8) Children and Minimum Age

NodeWorks is not directed to children under 13 and is intended for users who meet Discord's minimum age and Terms of Service requirements.
